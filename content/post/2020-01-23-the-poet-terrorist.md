words wound.</br>
phrases fillet.

like bombs they burst the heaving heart</br>
and shrapnel-shred</br>
the decency of slumber.

verbs vivisect,</br>
their violence vast.</br>

and one naught notices what nouns name</br>
until the now</br>
there is no unknowing.</br>
